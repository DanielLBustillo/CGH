ABOUT


Cannibal Grandpa is a young extreme metal formation from Madrid (Spain) which combines different elements of Death Metal such as Slam or Brutal Death Metal with dark Black Metal riffs, in addition to gathering the most actual influences from hardcore (Usually tagged as Slamming Deathcore). Cannibal Grandpa was founded in early 2014 releasing the first demo on YouTube containing the very first 2 songs. This allowed them to introduce themselves as a new band in their hometown scene. They soon started playing all around the city with different bands from all over the country. In October of 2015 the band releases their first album by through all digital platforms edited by themselves. This album is considered a millestone due to the huge impact it had in both national and international levels. At just one year from the begining the band starts playing shows all over the country while spreding the word outside Spain with their album. Opening for bigger bands like Rise of The Northstar or Here Comes The Kraken or playing huge festivals like Resurrection Fest with Iron Maiden were achieved during this album period. Also the band reaches a third place in the legendary Wacken Open Air Metal Battle with more than 150 bands from all over the country. This stage comes to an end with their first european tour in 2017 reaching countrys like France, Belgium or The Netherlands.This album puts the band in one of the tops of deathcore bands in the country. Right after this tour the band starts recording their 2nd album. Halfway to september 2017 Cannibal Grandpa releases their album Septum Signa Inferno through Slam Worldwide. Digital and physical distribution was handled by Chugcore in United States, Hollowcrown Records in the UK and Nakerband for Europe. At this point the band goes another step further with their style, incorporating more death metal lines to their music, as much as dark riffs and evil melodies. With this album the band gets definitely a place in the international scene being the most recogniced “deathcore” band in the country. In October of 2017 the band starts the Septum Signa Inferno Tour releasing the album all over Spain and Europe with more than 50 shows played in countries like Germany, France, Belgium, Switzerland, Poland or Czech Republic. Played shows with bands like Carnifex or Stray from The Path, and festivals like legendary Download Festival in Madrid with Scorpions, Slipknot and Tool, DeathFeast in Germany with Dying Fetus and The Black Dahlia Murder or Fall In The Brawl Festival in the UK with Ingested and Acrania. Cannibal Grandpa signs with the mighty american booking agency Artery Global for European Booking in early 2018. Due to several problems acording with the agency the 1 and a half month tour with american deathcore band Angelmaker got cancelled as well as our headlining tour in the east of europe. “EVIL” is our most elaborated, mature and powerful album ever. It´s a new path for Death Metal listeners never listened before. Combining the fresh 90´s and 2000´s death metal sound with electronic music and modern black metal all over a malevolent orchestra playing evil symphonies, the band reaches his own unique style with no tags at all. Cannibal Grandpa is the story of the american serial killer Albert Fish and some of the atrocities he commited telled in every album. In this one we wanted to represent the evil as a concept in the most raw and extreme form possible. Albert Fish is the humanization of Evil in the modern world. Every song tells a different evil story, some of them are real based stories and some others are more ficticial, but all stories have something real and really evil. Albert Fish passed away in the electric chair and her son Alben Fish continued his legacy with the cannibal cult so in this concept album we wanted to reflect that evil never disappears even though his human form does. This album has been produced, recorded, mixed and mastered by the guitarist of the band Alex Tena. All videos were produced and edited by the drummer of the band Rubén Contreras. (As we always do for every album)
